  MOROCCO, DAY 3 (11/25/18)

  PREFACE

Yay, I made my own website from scratch. Sure I didn't get around to it until a year after the trip to Morocco (Morocco was in November 2018, and I put this together in October 2019), but better late than never. Facebook and Instagram, eat your heart out.
I had it all planned out.  Create my first website, experience Morocco, then upload the photos to my website.  Simple, right?  Well, my job spins me in a tornado sometimes, and before I new it, I no longer had time to finish building the site prior to the trip.  When I arrived back home, I wanted to create the content, but the thought of having to nitpick at the website infrastructure first no longer appealed to me.  On top of that, I had an issue with my photos duplicating themselves for some reason, and that tornado in my life was as relentless as ever.  The project slipped away.

Now, one year later (October 2019), I decided to finally put it all together.  Sure, the details of my trip to Morocco are not as fresh as they were a year ago, but it was a nice trip, and I have some great pictures.  This may be a simple website, but it's my own.  Facebook and Instagram, eat your heart out.

Now about that trip...
